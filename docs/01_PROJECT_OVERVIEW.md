# KanonOS – Project Overview

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Active  
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-08

---

# Vision

KanonOS is the daily workspace for sports coaches.

Our goal is not to build another sports management platform.

Our goal is to become the operating system where coaches plan, execute, evaluate and continuously improve their work.

Everything starts with the coach.

---

# Mission

Help coaches spend less time managing information and more time coaching.

KanonOS centralises planning, training, evaluation and team management into one simple and intuitive workspace.

---

# The Problem

Most coaches work with disconnected tools:

- Paper
- Word
- Excel
- Google Drive
- WhatsApp
- Notes

This creates duplicated work, lost information and inconsistent planning.

Knowledge disappears every season.

---

# Our Solution

KanonOS organises the coach's daily workflow.

Instead of creating isolated documents, coaches build connected knowledge.

Planning leads to training.

Training generates evaluation.

Evaluation improves future planning.

---

# Product Philosophy

The platform follows six principles.

## Coach First

The coach is always the primary user.

Future modules for coordinators, parents and athletes will always support the coach's work.

---

## One Entry Only

Information should only be entered once.

Everything else should reuse existing data.

---

## Planning Before Execution

Every training session should exist because of a defined objective.

Planning is the foundation of coaching.

---

## Simplicity Wins

The platform should remove complexity, not create it.

Every screen must answer one clear question.

---

## Modular Growth

The MVP solves one real problem.

Future versions expand functionality without changing the user's workflow.

---

## AI Assists

Artificial Intelligence supports coaches.

It never replaces coaching decisions.

# MVP

The first release focuses exclusively on coaches.

Included modules:

- Workspace
- Teams
- Weekly Planning
- Training Session
- Training Review
- History

---

# Long-Term Vision

The platform will progressively expand to include:

- Advanced Planning
- Technical Coordinators
- Parents
- Athletes
- AI Assistant
- External Integrations

---

# Success Criteria

The MVP succeeds when a coach can replace paper, Word and Excel during an entire training week using only KanonOS.
