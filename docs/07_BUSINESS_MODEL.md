# KanonOS – Business Model

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Active  
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-08

---

# Purpose

This document defines the business vision behind KanonOS.

The goal is to build a sustainable SaaS platform that becomes the daily operating system for sports coaches.

---

# Problem

Most coaches use multiple disconnected tools to manage their work.

Typical workflow:

- Paper
- Word
- Excel
- Google Drive
- WhatsApp
- Personal notes

This creates duplicated work, lost information and inconsistent planning.

---

# Solution

KanonOS centralises coaching into one connected workspace.

The platform helps coaches:

- Plan
- Execute
- Review
- Learn
- Improve

Everything is connected through a single workflow.

---

# Target Market

Primary Market

- Team sports coaches

Secondary Markets

- Technical Coordinators
- Sports Clubs
- Sports Academies
- Federations

Future Expansion

The architecture is designed to support multiple sports through configurable planning models.

---

# Ideal Customer

A coach who:

- Plans training weekly
- Uses multiple digital tools
- Wants to save time
- Values organisation
- Wants to improve coaching quality

---

# Value Proposition

KanonOS replaces fragmented coaching tools with one simple workspace.

Benefits:

- Less administration
- Better planning
- Connected information
- Historical knowledge
- Better coaching decisions

---

# Revenue Model

Subscription (SaaS)

Possible plans:

### Free

- Limited teams
- Basic planning

### Coach

- Unlimited planning
- Training review
- History
- AI assistance

### Club

- Multiple coaches
- Coordinator tools
- Shared planning
- Reports

Enterprise solutions may be offered in the future.

---

# Competitive Advantage

KanonOS is not another club management platform.

It is built around the coach's daily workflow.

Key differentiators:

- Planning-first approach
- Coach Workspace
- Connected planning architecture
- Historical coaching knowledge
- AI as an assistant
- Modular growth

---

# Go-to-Market

Phase 1

Validate the MVP with a small group of coaches.

Phase 2

Launch publicly for individual coaches.

Phase 3

Expand to clubs and coordinators.

Phase 4

Scale internationally.

---

# Success Metrics

The business succeeds when coaches:

- Use KanonOS every training week.
- Replace external planning tools.
- Recommend the platform to other coaches.
- Upgrade to paid plans because it saves time and improves their work.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Become the reference operating system for sports coaching worldwide.

Every great season should start with KanonOS.
